We stand in the gap for under-served kids who don't have access to products or life saving medications. We deeply appreciate your kind donations!

Gap Fillers Flathead is a 501(c)(3) charitable organization. 100% of your donation stays in the Greater Flathead Valley for area schools.

Gap Fillers Flathead provides funds to local schools for three crucial areas of need:

1) Students who have incurred lunch debt.

2) Providing lifesaving medical equipment such as AEDs and lifesaving medications such as Epi-Pens.

3) Feminine dispensers and products for girls who do not have access to these supplies or are afraid to ask.
